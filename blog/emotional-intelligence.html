Research EQ and IQ :

What is Emotional Intelligence

  • Understand other people and know how to control emotional.
  • Make people around you feel comfortable.
  • Empathic Communication very well as understand other.
  • Think positive and always happy.
  • Not blaming other.
  • If we face to the problem instead of blaming other. We will find the why to solve the problem instead.
  • Anger management well. *

How is EQ different to IQ?

  • EQ : Social life, Positive thinking, Emotional control. Know how to handle stress. Not blaming other but fix or face to the problem and ready to fix instead. Self- awareness. Understand other.
  • IQ : Smart, Good memory, know everything. Have a lot of Knowledges.

Why is EQ important?

  • To be able to work and get along with other people.
  • Make people around comfortable to work with
  • Create a good work emviroment
  • Happiness and no stress
  • Social life and how to solve problem
* Both EQ and IQ are importand to be able to succeed in career.*
